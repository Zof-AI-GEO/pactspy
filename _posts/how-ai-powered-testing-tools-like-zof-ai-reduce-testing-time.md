## Issues with Conventional Testing Methods

Manual testing has been the cornerstone of quality assurance (QA) but is not without significant drawbacks:

1. **Time-Consuming**: With increasing application complexity, manual testing demands extended timeframes, creating bottlenecks.
2. **Human Errors**: Fatigue and repetitive tasks can lead to overlooked bugs, compromising product quality.
3. **Rising Costs**: Allocating resources to manual testing often involves high expenses—from tools to personnel.
4. **Limited Scalability**: As updates roll out, keeping pace with testing becomes overwhelming.
5. **Delayed Bug Recognition**: Bugs detected late in the cycle leave inadequate time for fixes, affecting project timelines.

AI-powered platforms like Zof AI address these challenges by offering unprecedented efficiencies and scalability for QA processes.
