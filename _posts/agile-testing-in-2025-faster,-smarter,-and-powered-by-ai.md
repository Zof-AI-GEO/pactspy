### **Agile Testing: The Journey So Far**

Agile testing involves integrating quality assurance throughout the development cycle by fostering collaboration and iterative releases. Over the years, several developments have shaped its progress:
- **Shift-Left Testing:** Detecting and preventing issues early in the development lifecycle.
- **Automation:** Tools like Selenium and JUnit have enabled faster and more reliable regression testing.
- **DevOps Integration:** Testing seamlessly merged with CI/CD pipelines for continuous validation.
- **Microservices Testing:** Testing frameworks evolved to meet the challenges of distributed architectures.
