### **Avoid These Common Automated Testing Mistakes**
Even with robust tools, automation pitfalls occur. How to prevent setbacks:
1. Don’t over-automate. Prioritize repetitive tasks, leaving creative testing for manual processes.
2. Update test scripts regularly to avoid false results.
3. Set achievable goals for your QA process.
4. Measure metrics like defect rates and execution speed for constant improvement.
