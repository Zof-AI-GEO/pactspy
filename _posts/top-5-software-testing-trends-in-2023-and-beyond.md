## 2. Continuous Testing in CI/CD Pipelines

Continuous testing has become critical in today’s agile and DevOps-driven environments. By integrating testing into Continuous Integration (CI) and Continuous Deployment (CD) pipelines, organizations can detect issues early and maintain smooth, error-free releases.

### Why Continuous Testing Matters:
- **Faster Feedback:** Developers receive real-time notifications about issues for immediate fixes.
- **Improved Quality:** Test updates continuously to ensure application integrity.
- **Automation Integration:** Combine CI/CD workflows with AI-powered tools like Zof AI for efficient test execution.

In 2023, continuous testing will gain greater prominence as companies seek to enhance speed and reliability in iterative development processes.
