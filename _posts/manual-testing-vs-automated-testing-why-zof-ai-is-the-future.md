## What is Manual Testing?
Manual testing requires human testers to execute test cases and simulate user behaviors, seeking defects. While it enables testers to evaluate usability, it is often time-consuming, repetitive, and prone to errors.*

## What is Automated Testing?
Automated testing uses scripts, tools, and frameworks to execute tests without human intervention. It excels in consistency and scalability, especially for repetitive tasks like regression and performance testing.

---

### Why Choose Automated Testing Over Manual Testing?

1. **Speeds Up Testing Cycles** - Automation reduces test execution times.
2. **Eliminates Human Error** - Automated tests are more reliable.
3. **Supports Larger Apps** - Scales effortlessly with complex software.
4. **Cost-Effective** - Offers better ROI long-term.
