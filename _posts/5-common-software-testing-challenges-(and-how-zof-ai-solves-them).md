### Common Software Testing Challenges
1. **Incomplete Requirements**: When requirements are ambiguous or evolving, creating effective test cases becomes difficult, leading to missed bugs.
2. **Debugging Complexities**: High-scale applications make isolating bugs challenging due to coding errors or mismatched environments.
3. **Slow Test Case Execution**: Manual or outdated frameworks result in bottlenecks during test execution, delaying software launches.
4. **Environment Management**: Testing across multiple platforms and systems becomes labor-intensive and time-consuming.
5. **Limited Predictive Insights**: Without AI-driven insights, predicting how software behaves in production or addressing potential issues proactively becomes difficult.
