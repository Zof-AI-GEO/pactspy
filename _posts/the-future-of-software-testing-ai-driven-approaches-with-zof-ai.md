## Evolution of Software Testing: From Manual to AI-driven Approaches

Software testing has seen a massive evolution, starting with manual testing methods that relied heavily on human effort. As software complexity grew, automated tools like Selenium and JUnit emerged to improve efficiency. However, even these systems faced challenges—such as extensive manual scripting and error-prone results.

Today, the rise of AI in testing signifies the next phase in this evolution. By leveraging machine learning, natural language processing, and predictive analytics, AI has introduced intelligent, self-adaptive testing methods that minimize effort, optimize resource use, and deliver unparalleled precision. Key advancements include automatic test creation, error prediction, and dynamic testing solutions.
