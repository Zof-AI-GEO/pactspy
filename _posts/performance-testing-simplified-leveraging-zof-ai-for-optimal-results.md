## Understanding Performance Testing and Its Importance

Performance testing evaluates application speed, scalability, stability, and reliability under specific workloads. It ensures seamless performance, reducing risks of downtime, user dissatisfaction, and reputational damage.

### Objectives of Performance Testing:
1. **Speed**: Real-time responses with minimal delays.
2. **Scalability**: Prove it can handle increasing user loads.
3. **Stability**: Operate efficiently under stress or peak conditions.
