## Major Challenges in Manual Testing

### 1. **Lengthy Processes**
Manually navigating complex systems and features can be exceedingly time-consuming, affecting project timelines.

### 2. **Human Errors Are Inevitable**
Manual testers might unintentionally overlook critical defects, leading to bugs in production and hurting user satisfaction.

### 3. **Scaling is a Struggle**
Testing extensive, interdependent systems overwhelms manual teams, making scaling an uphill battle.

### 4. **Inconsistent Outcomes**
Testing with different people can result in inconsistency, affecting the software's quality.

### 5. **Expensive Over Time**
Time-heavy processes require higher human effort, leading to increased long-term costs for organizations.

While these issues emphasize manual testing's limitations, Zof AI steps in to empower testers with cutting-edge solutions.
