## Why Traditional QA Needs a Makeover

Outdated QA processes, whether manual or automated, pose major obstacles:

1. **Time-Intensive Manual Testing**: Manually validating functionality across a range of platforms and devices is both slow and unsustainable, especially in fast-paced Agile or DevOps environments.
2. **High Automation Maintenance Costs**: Automated scripts need continuous updates, adding significant strain to QA teams.
3. **Human Error**: Repetitive testing tasks often lead to fatigue and missed defects.
4. **Scalability Problems**: Managing complex integrations and user workflows at scale overwhelms traditional testing methods.
5. **Delayed Results**: Manual testing often results in feedback that arrives too late in the development lifecycle, increasing costs when bugs are identified post-development.

AI-based platforms like Zof.ai are reshaping the QA landscape to address these crucial challenges and move beyond the limitations of traditional methods.
