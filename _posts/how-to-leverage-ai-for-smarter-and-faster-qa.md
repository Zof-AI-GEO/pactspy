## What is AI-Driven Software Testing?

AI-driven software testing employs AI and machine learning (ML) to streamline test creation, execution, and analysis, going beyond traditional methods. AI systems analyze large datasets, identify testing patterns, and optimize decision-making for enhanced efficiency.

### Benefits of AI-Driven Testing:
1. **Improved Accuracy**: AI identifies anomalies using historical data and predictive models, reducing human errors.
2. **Scalability**: AI scales effortlessly to handle vast, complex applications.
3. **Faster Testing**: Cognitive learning and predictive analytics accelerate test execution and issue analysis.
4. **Seamless Continuous Testing**: AI automates testing for smoother DevOps integration during continuous development.
