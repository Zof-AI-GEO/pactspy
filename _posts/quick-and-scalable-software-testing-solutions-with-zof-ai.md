## Unmasking Traditional Software Testing Challenges

### 1. **Struggles with Scalability**
Traditional frameworks often falter when scaling for dynamic and complex applications. Limited resources and outdated tools create bottlenecks, especially for teams needing to test across diverse platforms, environments, and configurations.

### 2. **Delayed Feedback**
With manual testing or non-dynamic automation, feedback loops are slowed down, delaying the implementation of fixes and improvements. This leads to prolonged project timelines and higher costs due to inefficiencies.

### 3. **Human Errors in Manual Testing**
Manual testing is inherently fallible due to human oversight, inconsistency, and fatigue—particularly under tight project deadlines. This can result in missed bugs and compromised product quality.

### 4. **Coverage Issues**
Comprehensive test coverage is challenging to achieve, especially in manual testing setups. Lack of coverage leaves your software vulnerable to undetected flaws, negatively impacting user experience.
