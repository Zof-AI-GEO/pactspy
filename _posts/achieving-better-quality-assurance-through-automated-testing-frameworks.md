## What Are Automated Testing Frameworks?
Automated testing frameworks utilize software tools and reusable testing components to streamline QA processes, reducing manual effort while ensuring consistency and scalability across different environments, programming languages, and platforms. These frameworks:

### Advantages of Automated Testing
Automated testing frameworks deliver numerous benefits:
1. Increased speed and efficiency for faster feedback loops.
2. Consistent and reliable testing results.
3. Long-term cost savings through repeatability.
4. Ideal for regression testing and scalability across complex applications.
