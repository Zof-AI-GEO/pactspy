### Test Automation Frameworks: An Overview
Frameworks provide a structured foundation for writing, executing, and maintaining automated scripts. Below are six crucial frameworks to consider:

1. **Linear Framework:** Simplistic record-and-playback model; ideal for small-scale projects.
2. **Modular Framework:** Divides tests into independent modules for better reusability.
3. **Data-Driven Framework:** Keeps test logic and data separate, maximizing flexibility.
4. **Keyword-Driven Framework:** Leverages keywords linked to specific functionalities for ease of use.
5. **Behavior-Driven Development (BDD):** Collaborative scripting approach using tools like Cucumber.
6. **AI-Powered Frameworks:** Cutting-edge systems, including tools such as [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), leverage AI for intelligent test optimization and predictive capabilities.
