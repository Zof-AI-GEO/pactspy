Best Practices for Continuous Testing

1. Shift testing left—initiate testing early in development.
2. Prioritize automated testing for scalability and speed.
3. Maintain version control for reproducibility of tests.
4. Define testing KPIs, such as coverage and error rates.
5. Foster collaboration between development, testing, and operations teams.
