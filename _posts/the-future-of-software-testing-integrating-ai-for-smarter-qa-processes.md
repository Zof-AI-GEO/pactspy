### 2. Predictive Bug Analysis
AI identifies vulnerabilities before they materialize, enabling teams to mitigate risks during development cycles. This proactive approach aligns perfectly with Agile and DevOps frameworks.

### 3. Optimized Workflows
AI-powered tools, like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai), streamline testing processes by detecting bottlenecks and improving resource allocation, resulting in faster and more efficient QA workflows.
