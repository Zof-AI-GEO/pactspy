## What is AI-Powered Software Testing?

AI-powered software testing integrates artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies to improve testing's accuracy, speed, and adaptability. Unlike traditional testing methods dependent on manual scripting, AI reduces labor by identifying patterns, predicting weaknesses, and generating advanced test cases.

- **Increased Efficiency**: Automates repetitive tasks so testers can focus on complex test cases.
- **Improved Accuracy**: Reduces human error during test execution and reporting.
- **Rapid Test Execution**: Operates tests continuously for faster development cycles.
- **Dynamic Adaptability**: Learns and evolves with your software to adopt changes in real time.
