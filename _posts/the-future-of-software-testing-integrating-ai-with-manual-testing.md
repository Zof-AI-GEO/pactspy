### 1. **Faster Bug Detection**
AI excels in identifying vulnerabilities at unprecedented speeds, while manual testers focus on aesthetics and nuanced functionality.

AI simulates diverse environments, combining with manual review to finalize user-centric features.

### 3. **Increased Efficiency**
AI automates repetitive tasks, freeing testers to address complex scenarios and innovation.

### 4. **Minimized Human Error**
AI consistency eradicates common mistakes while providing reliable predictive insights.

### 5. **Actionable Decision-Making**
Data-driven predictions and insights from AI inform manual testers, elevating decision accuracy.
