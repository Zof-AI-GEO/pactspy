## Top Trends in AI-Driven Software Testing
AI adoption in software testing has skyrocketed thanks to its ability to automate and optimize complex QA processes. Here are key trends shaping the future of testing:

1. **AI-Driven Test Automation**: Automated test cases enhance speed, precision, and reduce costly errors.
2. **Self-Healing Scripts**: AI tools adapt to changing system environments, ensuring smooth testing workflows.
3. **Continuous Integration Testing**: Meeting the demands of agile and DevOps requires constant, real-time quality assurance.
4. **Predictive Defects Analysis**: Machine learning algorithms identify potential bugs before they cause major issues.
5. **Smart Defect Mining**: Automates bug identification, prioritizes fixes, and leverages AI-driven insights for smarter software enhancement.
